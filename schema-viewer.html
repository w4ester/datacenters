üéØ Vision: Educational AI Sovereignty

The Problem

  • Student data feeds centralized AI systems
  • Schools don't own their trained models
  • Privacy and sovereignty concerns
  • No local control over educational data

The Solution

  • Schools host AI models locally at nearby data centers
  • Train models on their own student data
  • Own the resulting models completely
  • Data never leaves their region
  • Community-controlled infrastructure

This Map Enables

  • Find nearby facilities with education pricing
  • Identify data sovereignty-compliant options
  • Compare costs and sustainability metrics
  • Plan local AI infrastructure for schools
  • "Carbon capture for educational data" - keeping student-generated data working for students, not corporations

5 Supported Use Cases

  • Sustainability Analysis: Environmental impact, PUE, renewable energy, water usage, backup power infrastructure
  • Investment & Planning: Market analysis, construction pipeline, occupancy rates, tenant concentration risk
  • Network Infrastructure: Connectivity mapping, latency optimization, peering strategy
  • Education Data Sovereignty: Local AI hosting, student data ownership, cost-effective options, capacity planning
  • Operational Intelligence: Generator dependency, energy source diversification, rack utilization, tenant analysis, backup power readiness
